# 🌾 KissanConnect – Smart Agricultural Portal for Nepal

> **Empowering Nepali Farmers Through Digital Innovation**

KissanConnect is a modern, user-friendly agricultural platform designed to bridge the gap between farmers, buyers, agricultural experts, and government services in Nepal. The platform provides farmers with essential tools, real-time information, and digital services to improve productivity, increase income, and simplify access to agricultural resources.

---

## 📖 About the Project

Agriculture is the backbone of Nepal's economy, yet many farmers still struggle to access reliable market prices, weather information, government subsidies, modern farming knowledge, and digital marketplaces.

**KissanConnect** was developed to solve these challenges by bringing multiple agricultural services together into a single digital platform.

Whether a farmer wants to sell crops, check the weather, learn modern farming techniques, calculate government subsidies, or identify crop diseases using AI, KissanConnect provides everything in one place.
